Through the Ages

India's Historical Eras

Journey through 5,000 years of civilization, empire, and artistry.

Ancient India

3000 BCE

Indus Valley Civilization — Mohenjo-daro and Harappa establish the world's first planned cities.

Maurya Empire

322 BCE

Ashoka spreads Buddhism across Asia, leaving a legacy of rock edicts and the Sanchi Stupa.

Gupta Period

320 CE

India's golden age of science, art, and literature. The Ajanta Caves reach their pinnacle.

Mughal Era

1526 CE

Babur founds the Mughal Empire. Shah Jahan builds the Taj Mahal as an eternal monument to love.

Colonial Period

1858 CE

British Raj begins. The Gateway of India rises in Bombay as a symbol of imperial rule.

Modern India

1947 CE

Independence. India inherits the world's richest repository of historical monuments.

Tell me about the Taj Mahal.
The Taj Mahal was commissioned by Shah Jahan in 1632 CE as a mausoleum for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Built over 22 years by 20,000 artisans, it represents the zenith of Mughal architecture — combining Persian, Islamic, and Indian styles. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Best visited at sunrise when the marble glows pink. ✨
